  <h2 class="h1 h1--section">Chat on SDK</h2>

        <div class="has-border has-shadow paragraph paragraph--grid" style="border-color: rgba(255,255,255,1); background-color: rgba(255,255,255,1);">
              
      <div class="grid grid--2 paragraph--grid__elements">
              <div class="col--tablet--6 paragraph--grid__element">  <div class="paragraph paragraph--text">
          
            <div class="has-button has-list field field--field-text field--label-hidden field__item"><p><strong>AI Chat Support</strong> is available on SDK pages for all products on the Shell Developer Portal.&nbsp;</p>
<p>It is designed to help developers find information about SDK setup, usage, and endpoints, and provides code samples in Java, .NET, TypeScript, PHP, Python, and Ruby.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The chat uses details from OpenAPI specifications and is language- and product-specific.&nbsp;</p>
<p>You can access it by navigating to the SDK page for your chosen product and language and clicking <strong>Ask AI</strong>.</p>
